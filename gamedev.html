What is p5.js?

p5.js is a JavaScript library that starts with the original goal of Processing, to make coding accessible for artists, designers, educators, and beginners, and reinterprets this for today's web.

What is Heart Chase all about?

The concept is very simple. Stay away from the guys that are trying to finesse you and catch the hearts. In relation to real life, life is so much more simple when you stay away from guys trying to finesse you, and your chasing things that make your soul happy (the hearts). This game is suitable for all ages. Version .1.0

Finessed. Fin-ess-d. verb.
1: Meaning to steal from someone or to scam them.
2: To outplay someone.
